Private enough to read freely.

Motif runs in Chrome, keeps your vocabulary on your machine, and processes speech and transcription locally — so your reading stays yours after a one-time setup.

  • Natural speech Hear French pronounced in a natural voice, synthesized on your device — not sent to a cloud API.

  • Live transcription Transcribe audio from the tab you're watching, processed locally as you listen.

  • Saved locally Your vocabulary, notes, and page contexts stay in browser storage on your machine.

Initial model download requires network access. Powered by Supertonic, stt-web, Tesseract, and Chrome Translator.
